Apna HUB!

Apna HUB Pvt Ltd. is an Indian electronic commerce company based in Pune, India. Founded by Pralay Patil and Shardul Pattewar in January 2019, the company initially focused on gadget sales. The service competes primarily with Amazon's Indian subsidiary, and the domestic rival Snapdeal. As of April 2019, Apna HUB held a 18.5% market share of India's e-commerce industry. Apna HUB is significantly dominant in the sale of gadgets and was described as being "neck and neck" with Amazon in the sale of electronics and mobile phones.
